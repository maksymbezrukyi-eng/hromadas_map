## Working Rules For v0.5

- Do not change functionality during v0.5.
- Move code 1:1 first, without architecture improvements.
- Do not rename fields in the `H` array.
- Do not change DOM element `id` values.
- Do not change paths to GeoJSON files.
- Do not remove Google Sheets sync.
- Do not add new modules outside the target structure without a separate request.
- Check the diff after each step.
- Work in small steps.
- Before any edit, briefly explain what will be changed.
